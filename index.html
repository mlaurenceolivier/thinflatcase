Michael L. Olivier: Senior UI/UX Designer

I am a front-end designer who occasionally writes back-end code.

I was originally hired on as a Senior Designer for Gravity, which was acquired by Aol in 2014.

After the acquisition of Aol by Verizon, I became a Senior UI/UX Designer and began working with the Athena Publishing team, which became the Oath Publishing Team. Late 2017, the team was restructured and I was moved to work as a designer for the Pick-n-Roll and Slick teams.

I occasionally lend a hand to other teams, such as MAW and Data Sketches.
